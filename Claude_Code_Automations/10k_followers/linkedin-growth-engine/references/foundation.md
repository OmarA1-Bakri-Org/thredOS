## 1. Mission Statement Creation

Your LinkedIn presence must solve ONE specific problem. Everything flows from this.

### Using Mission-GPT
1. Go to: chatgpt.com/g/g-67530e1fbcbc819186a9cae1fc734cfe-mission-gpt
2. Click "Start" to begin.
3. Answer the GPT's clarifying questions about your expertise and audience.
4. It generates 10 ultra-compact mission options (4-6 words each).
   Example output: "Technique-first speaking for CEOs", "Train CEOs to own stages",
   "Turn leaders into practiced storytellers"
5. Pick the one that hits home, or ask it to tweak.
6. The GPT will also offer to run Color Theory GPT for your brand palette.

### Manual Method
Ask yourself:
- What is the ONE problem I solve?
- Who has this problem?
- What transformation do I deliver?

Format: "I help [WHO] do [WHAT] so they can [OUTCOME]"

### Validation Test
- Does it describe ONE problem, not a list of services?
- Can you explain it to a stranger in 10 seconds?
- Would someone with this problem immediately recognise it?

## 2. ICP Definition

Target ONE specific person. Every post should feel like it was written for them.

### Using ICP-GPT
1. Go to: chatgpt.com/g/g-89NEaQgUI-icp-gpt
2. Answer the guided questions.
3. Output: a detailed persona.

### Manual Method
Define:
- **Name** (make them real)
- **Age and gender**
- **Job title and industry**
- **Location**
- **Specific struggle** (what keeps them up at night?)
- **Aspiration** (what do they want to become?)
- **Current behaviour** (where do they look for solutions?)

### Example
> Sophia, 40, female, independent management consultant in Europe.
> Wants to position herself as an AI-upskilling expert but doesn't know
> where to start. Feels overwhelmed by the pace of AI change. Needs
> clear, jargon-free guidance she can implement immediately.

### The Daily Test
Before every post: "Would [ICP name] stop scrolling for this?"

## 3. Profile Optimisation Checklist

Complete all items before publishing your first post.

### Profile Picture
- 1080x1080 px
- Zoomed so face fills the frame
- Smiling noticeably
- Own a distinct colour for instant recognition
- See Section 4 for AI generation workflow

### Banner
- 1584x396 px
- Single clear message reinforcing your mission
- LinkedIn Premium enables slideshow banners

### Headline
- Mission-driven, not job-title-driven
- Cheeky and clear
- Good: "Master AI before it masters you"
- Bad: "AI Consultant | Speaker | Author"

### Featured Section
- One link only
- Big text, no descriptions
- Direct clicks matter more than explanations

### About Section
- Keep it minimal
- Extensive experience sections rank higher in LinkedIn search

### Brand Colours (Color Theory GPT)
Use the Color Theory GPT (linked from Mission-GPT) to generate a full brand palette:
- It produces Primary, Secondary, and Accent colours with HEX, HSL, and rationale.
- Example output: Primary "#07596B" (Courage Teal), Secondary "#B23C2E" (Brick Resolve),
  Accent "#5F4B8B" (Insight Purple).
- Apply these colours consistently to your banner, infographics, and carousel slides.

### Premium (Optional but Recommended)
- Enables special link placement under your name
- Improved conversion from profile visitor to follower

### Verification Test
Open your profile in an incognito browser. In 3 seconds, can a stranger
answer: "What does this person do and why should I follow them?"

## 4. AI Headshot Generation Workflow

### Google Gemini Method (Recommended)
1. Select a clear personal photo (good lighting, face visible).
2. Browse LinkedIn for a headshot style you admire. Screenshot it.
3. Open Google Gemini (gemini.google.com).
4. Upload both images.
5. Prompt: "Generate a corporate headshot of my image in [describe your clothing] in the same style as the 2nd image I uploaded as inspiration"
6. Download result.
7. Remove AI metadata:
   - Option A: Upload to aimetadatacleaner.com, download cleaned version
   - Option B: Take a screenshot of the result (screenshots carry no generation metadata)
   - Option C: Open in Canva, export as new file
8. Crop to 1080x1080 px.
9. Upload to LinkedIn.

### Alternative Tools
- **Flux**: Alternative AI image generator mentioned in guides
- **Nano Banana**: Recommended for content and photo generation
