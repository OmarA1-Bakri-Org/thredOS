# Compliance and Hard Rules

These rules apply to ALL outreach and reply drafting across every channel (email and LinkedIn). No exceptions.

## Universal Rules

1. **NEVER discuss pricing, commercial terms, or contractual details** — reserved for Solve/Negotiate stage
2. **NEVER make commitments** ("we can do X for you", "we'll integrate with Y")
3. **NEVER share confidential information** about other clients, partnerships, or pipeline
4. **NEVER respond to recruiters or spam**
5. **Email orchestrators act as Omar's delegated reviewer** — auto-send is allowed only when the draft passes all rules in `shared_references/email-review-guardrails.md`; exception cases must be escalated
6. **NEVER claim settlement finality or guaranteed delivery times** without T1 evidence (signed contract or published service spec). Use "near-instant" or "designed for real-time settlement" as safer alternatives
7. **NEVER include pricing** in outbound messages
8. **NEVER state a prospect's regulatory status** unless confirmed via regulatory register or their own public disclosures
9. If unsure about classification, flag it for Omar's review
10. If a prospect asks a technical question answerable from public TheGent info, answer briefly then pivot to a meeting
11. If a prospect asks something unanswerable, say "Great question — that's exactly what I'd want to walk through on a call. When works for you?"

## Email-Specific Rules

12. `OUTLOOK_SEND_DRAFT` may be used automatically only after the orchestrator completes delegated review and no exception trigger fires
13. NEVER reply-all unless Omar explicitly says to
14. Skip @thegent.uk emails — flag for Omar as internal
15. If multiple recipients (CC), note this so Omar can decide reply vs reply-all
16. For proactive outbound, if the Apollo owner is blank or Omar, outreach may proceed. If any other owner is set, outreach must be skipped
17. Exclude large incumbent banks and other special-handling accounts from automated outbound unless Omar explicitly whitelists them

## LinkedIn-Specific Rules

18. Never reveal AI involvement — messages should read as Omar's voice
19. No emojis in messages
20. Do NOT close the browser between operations — leave it open for the next batch or for sending
21. Do NOT hold commercial conversations in LinkedIn messages — the goal is to book a meeting, not negotiate
