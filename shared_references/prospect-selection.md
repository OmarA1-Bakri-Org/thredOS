# Prospect Selection Guide

## Priority Ranking

When selecting contacts for outreach, prioritise in this order:

1. **Due or overdue follow-ups** — existing outreach threads that need a response or nudge today
2. **Hot replies** — prospects who replied to previous outreach (highest intent signal)
3. **Stale deals** — open deals with no activity > 14 days (need a nudge)
4. **Closing soon** — deals closing within 7 days that need a push
5. **Comeback Queue** — contacts in the Nudger pipeline due for re-engagement
6. **New prospects** — fresh contacts from Apollo or enrichment that haven't been touched
7. **Cross-channel warm handoffs** — contacts flagged by the other channel's agent as engaged (from shared-intel)

## Account Fit Criteria

| Factor | High Priority | Low Priority |
|---|---|---|
| Regulatory status | Licensed PSP, EMI, bank, exchange | Unlicensed, unclear status |
| Corridor relevance | Active in TheGent focus corridors (ASIAPAC, EMEA) | Outside focus regions |
| Cross-border volume | >$100M annual | Small domestic-only |
| Tech stack | API-first, modern infrastructure | Legacy batch processing |
| Geographic presence | ASIAPAC or EMEA HQ | Americas-only |
| Account type | Mid-market PSPs, fintech infra, focused operators | Large incumbent banks, tier-1 global banks |

## Contact Fit Criteria

| Factor | Prioritise | Deprioritise |
|---|---|---|
| Decision authority | C-suite, VP, Director | IC, analyst |
| Prior engagement | Has replied, taken meetings, active thread | Never engaged |
| Budget influence | P&L owner, budget holder | No purchasing authority |

## Hard Exclusions

Do not send automated outreach if any of the following are true:

1. The company is a large incumbent bank or global tier-1 bank.
2. Apollo shows an owner other than Omar for the contact, company, or deal.
3. The account is on the special-handling list, including SentBe / SentB variants.
